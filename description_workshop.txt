[h1]Pet Bestiary[/h1]

Pet Bestiary adds a custom Bestiary-style menu for collecting, managing, and displaying multiple pets at once.

Once a pet item enters your inventory, that pet is unlocked for your character. From there, you can activate it through the Pet Bestiary without relying on Terraria’s normal single pet slot.

[h2]Features[/h2]

- Equip multiple normal pets and light pets at once
- Custom Bestiary-inspired pet UI
- Separate Normal Pet and Light Pet sections
- Unlock pets by obtaining their summon item
- Vanilla pet unlock hints
- Modded pet detection where possible
- Pet presets
- Equip All and Unequip All buttons
- Lock pets to protect them from bulk actions
- Optional progression mode for unlocking more pet slots over time
- Per-pet dye support for active in-world pets: Each pet can have its own assigned dye. Dye items are not consumed, moved, stored, or deleted. The mod only saves the dye effect assigned to that pet.
- Debug tools for testing and troubleshooting

[h2]Mod Compatibility[/h2]

Pet Bestiary attempts to detect pets from other mods automatically. Some modded pets may show ??? for unlock conditions if the source cannot be determined.

[h2]Known Limitations[/h2]

- The pet preview may not always show assigned dye yet.
- Some custom-drawn modded pets may not support dye perfectly.
- Pets may overlap because spacing behavior is not implemented yet.
- Some modded unlock conditions may be unknown.

[h2]Planned Features[/h2]

- Dyed pet preview support
- Visual pet spacing
- Optional duplicate pets
- Better modded pet compatibility
- More filtering/search tools
- More preset and progression polish

[h2]Safety[/h2]

Pet Bestiary does not use real extra pet equipment slots and does not store your pet or dye items. Everything is handled through a virtual pet system to avoid item loss. When I was originally making the more pet slots mod and trying to add the individual dye function, the custom dye slot would just eat your dye sometimes. I don't wish to make people lose their dyes so I've avoided that.
